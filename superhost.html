Who Is Karina Piha?

Karina Piha is a Brazilian artist with European and Arab roots, living in New Zealand. Her unique style reflects a blend of vibrant cultures, shaped by years living in Germany and other countries.

Fluent in multiple languages, including Portuguese, English, German, and Spanish, Karina brings a global perspective to her art. Her work is celebrated for its bold colors, emotional depth, and authenticity.

In addition to her artistic career, Karina offers art therapy and teaches Yoga Nidra and Ashtanga. Her home, a popular Airbnb destination, mirrors the warmth and creativity found in her artwork.
