About Casa da Karina

The Casa da Karina sleep out is located in the most noble area of Havelock North, the green belt, walking distance to the village, schools, shops and restaurants. You will have your own private room and bathroom away from the main house but inside our lovely garden.

Additionally you may enjoy the separate Yoga Room or even choose to book private classes with Karina. Havelock North is close to Mata Peak, wineries and beaches.

Enjoy Hawke’s Bay staying in the heart of Havelock North.

The space
You will find everything you need fora short stay with comfort: quality linen and towels, toiletries, internet, a mini fridge filled with treats for a basic breakfast plus a microwave in case you want to heat up a takeaway.

Guest access
Guests can use the garden, veranda and the yoga room.

During your stay
I am mostly around the main house and am happy to help with directions, reservations, or tips of what to do or where to go around Hawke’s Bay.

Other things to note
In case you have a well behaved pet they are always welcome to stay.
