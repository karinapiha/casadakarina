About Casa da Karina

Casa da Karina was built in 1932 as part of Muritai Homestead. Now it is my home, full of character and charm, perfect to welcome friends and family.

My dog Lola is only acting as a model but won’t be here during your stay. Our Cat however, stays.

In the so called "green circle" of Havelock North. It is a 7-10 minute stroll into the Village where all shops and restaurants are at walking distance: even Te Mata Peak.

Wineries and beaches are a short drive away. You’ll enjoy Casa da Karina.

The space
Casa da Karina has 4 comfortable bedrooms: 3 in the main house and an ensuite sleepout which is a separate listing you can add if needed: link.

Guest access
Two cars fit inside the gate. Additional parking is just outside the door.

Other things to note
I have a ginger cat called "Cat". She's moody and might not show up, but please keep her bowl full.

I also have a self-contained studio that can be booked too. Ask during booking if it will be occupied.
